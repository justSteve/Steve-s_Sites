22 Years, 10 Phases, 4 Technology Platforms

Total Training Solutions was a Wisconsin-based bank and credit union training company that operated from 1998 to 2020. What began as a CD-ROM training shop in Madison evolved through hand-coded HTML, ASP-driven pop-out menus, Flash animations, a WordPress CMS, and finally a full webinar platform serving financial institutions nationwide.

This page walks through each distinct phase of the company's online presence, using original images and text preserved by the Wayback Machine across 27 curated snapshots.

1998

The CD-ROM Era

First snapshot: December 1, 1998 · Madison, WI · P.O. Box 14518

The original ttstrain.com launched as a table-based HTML 3.2 page with a diamond-pattern tiled background. Navigation used CD-ROM disc icons that swapped between selected and unselected states via Netscape 3-era JavaScript image rollovers.

"Our clients vary from small companies to some of the largest organizations in the country. In addition to off-the-shelf banking programs, we serve other industries with custom development and consulting."

Products: CD-ROM training titles for financial institutions — teller fundamentals, compliance, IRA training, office ergonomics.

1999–2001

Early Growth

4 snapshots · Compliance Series arrives · Same basic design

The site kept its diamond-background aesthetic through early 2001, but the content grew steadily. The Compliance Series training product was introduced in 1999 (with a new indexComptitl.gif graphic). By 2001 the "CD-ROMs" label gave way to broader training categories and a dedicated Compliance demo button appeared.

The September 2001 snapshot, captured just after 9/11, added a usa.gif flag graphic. Content grew from 2,706 bytes to 3,071 bytes as new products were added.

2002–2004

The Professional Redesign

Full brand suite · Blue/gray scheme · ASP backend · Waunakee, WI

In early 2002 the site underwent its first major redesign. The diamond background and CD-ROM icons were replaced by a polished blue (#6699CC) and gray layout with an external CSS stylesheet, pop-out JavaScript menus, and an ASP backend. The company address changed from Madison to Waunakee, WI (PO Box 310, 53597).

This era introduced a full brand suite: name.gif (company name header), logo_an.gif (animated globe logo), tts.gif (abbreviated mark), and slogan.gif (tagline). The site also added decorative UI elements including corner pieces, separators, and a gray-lines background tile.

"Total Training Solutions offers a variety of training programs for banks and credit unions of all sizes. Train on the web with our on-line compliance or IRA training, allow us to develop custom internet/intranet training or develop efficiency with our CD-ROM based teller fundamental series."

Products: Web-Based Training, Webinars, Custom Training Programs, Videos and CD-ROMs. Compliance, IRA, and custom development emphasized.

2005–2007

The Stable ASP Period

Flash rotating logo · Pop-out JS menus · Stable content

By late 2003, a simplified logo_TTS.gif appeared, and by late 2005 a Flash rotating logo (tts_logo_rotate.swf) was embedded. The page size jumped from ~2,630 to ~3,584 bytes. Content and layout remained stable for nearly two years — the pop-out JavaScript menus persisted, and the blue/gray color scheme was unchanged.

This was TTS's longest period of stability online. The same basic page served visitors from November 2005 through May 2007, with only minor content tweaks between snapshots.

Note: The Flash .swf file was not captured by the Wayback Machine — only its HTML reference survives.

2007–2010

The Dark Period

Site breaks · 543 bytes · Redirect to w3.ttstrain.com

In June 2007, the site suddenly collapsed. The Wayback Machine captured only 543 bytes — a near-empty HTML shell containing a JavaScript redirect to http://w3.ttstrain.com/. The content area was completely blank.

<SCRIPT LANGUAGE="JavaScript">
this.location.href = "http://w3.ttstrain.com/";
</SCRIPT>

This redirect era lasted for over three years. No further meaningful content was captured at the root domain until January 2011. The w3 subdomain content was not archived.

2011

The Transition

Minimal page · 1,093 bytes · BankWebinars.com pivot

In January 2011, the site briefly returned — but only as a minimal landing page. The content was a centered box with the Flash TTSLogo.swf animation and a single link:

"Total Training Solutions Proudly Powers BankWebinars.com"

This marked a pivotal business moment. TTS had shifted from selling CD-ROM and web-based training directly to powering a dedicated webinar platform at BankWebinars.com. The ttstrain.com domain became essentially a redirect.

2013–2015

WordPress Revival

Modern CMS · jQuery accordion slider · 5 product panels · Moonstruck Media design

By February 2013, ttstrain.com was completely rebuilt as a custom CMS site (PHP-based, with jQuery 1.7.1). The design featured a dark navigation bar, newsletter signup box, and a jQuery accordion slider showcasing five product panels:

The page was now a content-rich portal with testimonials, an experts corner, upcoming events feed, ABA news articles, and a sticky footer. The design was credited to Moonstruck Media Production, LLC.

2016–2020

The Full Platform

WordPress 4.5 · Responsive theme · UberMenu · FlexSlider · HTTPS by 2017 · Final capture April 2020

In May 2016, the site underwent its biggest transformation — jumping from ~3,800 bytes to over 12,600 bytes. The new WordPress-powered site used the Responsive theme with a TTS child theme, UberMenu mega menus, FlexSlider hero carousels, Gravity Forms, and Font Awesome icons. The page was fully responsive with a Yoast SEO plugin and structured data markup.

The homepage featured a rotating hero banner with stock photography, three-column callout panels for each product line, live RSS-fed webinar listings from BankWebinars.com and CUWebinars.com, a testimonials carousel, and a Twitter feed widget.

Products:

  • Bank & Credit Union Webinars — via BankWebinars.com and CUWebinars.com
  • Directors Education Series — 20-25 minute board meeting modules
  • Compliance Perspectives — monthly compliance webinar series
  • Webinar Subscription Packages — unlimited access plans for banks and credit unions
"I have to say this has to be one of the best webinars I have ever attended!! Usually, I can barely keep my eyes open. I like how it had us interact with the webinar by filling in information."
—Lara E. Peterson, First National Bank Of Picayune

By October 2017, the site had transitioned to HTTPS. The final evolution of the logo came in 2018/2019 with TTS_logo_rev.png, and the product lineup expanded with dedicated brand logos for each sub-platform:

2020

Epilogue

After the April 2020 capture, the ttstrain.com domain went dark. The Wayback Machine recorded no further meaningful content.

Over 22 years, Total Training Solutions evolved from a Madison, Wisconsin CD-ROM training shop to a nationwide webinar platform serving banks and credit unions. The technology stack progressed from hand-coded HTML 3.2 with JavaScript image rollovers, through ASP with pop-out menus, Flash animation, custom PHP with jQuery, and finally a fully-equipped WordPress installation with responsive design, mega menus, and live content feeds.

The company's domain served seven distinct logo designs across four complete site redesigns. This archive preserves 27 curated Wayback Machine snapshots with 50 original image assets spanning the full arc of the company's online presence.
