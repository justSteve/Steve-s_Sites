Nestled in a cozy home office in the Coulee region of Western Wisconsin I've had the great good fortune to work with people all over the world by leveraging the power of a simple dialup internet connection. I've developed and maintained a productive dialog (both verbal and textual) with associates from the mountains of North Carolina, to California, Korea, Europe, and Australia.

Since 1994 I've been part of a number of teams and project groups that -meeting only online- have focused on exploring the opportunities afforded by internetted communication for business, educational and personal enrichment.

Electronic training environment development is the single most productive use of internet technologies and I count myself lucky to have the opportunity to have participated in the development of marketing strategies of several different companies. I'm looking forward to using what I've learned in the trenches.

Current Projects

Since November '96 I've been working with The Worldsmith (formerly the Lanning Group) on a Multi-User Virtual Environment. Our technology is unique in it's ability to allow a WWW user -without installing a separate client- to connect and interact in a fully HTML-enabled multi-user environment. These internet creatures are outstanding community building tools and knowledge representation/transfer resources.
