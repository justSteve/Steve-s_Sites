Greetings!

My focus on the internet has been how it changes our relationships and even our expectations of relationships.  As the net continues to permeate our systems of commerce, education, and civic/social interactions these changes will be all the more interesting. This site is part resume, part scrapbook, and part experimental workshop. Hosting such a site has allowed me to explore questions that go to the heart of the nature of communication and identity.

I hope you find your visit here productive, informative, and entertaining. Parallel to every other context this site presents - be it my professional services, sailing experiences, my wife's portfolio, my bookmarks, whatever - is the attempt to stimulate questions about the nature of this medium. Please feel free to use the link found at the bottom of each page to share your questions and reactions.
